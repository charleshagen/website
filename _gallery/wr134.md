In my processing of this target, just at the very edge near the top of the frame, I noticed a very faint smudge of Oiii. After some further investigation I found the star [], a white dwarf candidate star, directly in the center of the object. The nebula itself appears to be yet uncatalogued, not appearing in SIMBAD or HASH.
